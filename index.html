Africa Safer Internet Day (ASID) 2026

Africa Safer Internet Day 2026 was celebrated on 10 February 2026, with activities continuing throughout the month of February to promote child online protection and digital empowerment across Africa.

Coordinated in Lesotho by Skillshare Lesotho in partnership with the African Committee of Experts on the Rights and Welfare of the Child, ASID 2026 brings together governments, schools, parents, communities, media, and technology partners to protect children in the digital environment.

“Together for a Safer Africa Online: AU Online Safety Policy in Action”

The campaign supports implementation of the African Union Child Online Safety and Empowerment Policy, ensuring children can learn, connect, and grow online safely.

As internet use expands across Africa, children face risks such as cyberbullying, online abuse, harmful content, and privacy threats. ASID 2026 promotes awareness, education, stronger legal protection, and community action to safeguard every child online.

ASID 2026 is more than a celebration. It is a continental movement to build a safer digital Africa for every child.
